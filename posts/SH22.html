    16.7.22 - Going North

    Today, we dve about 600 km from the region of Frankfurt to the beautiful island Fehmarn. We depated at around 12:30 pm after enjoying the time with collegues of us. After departure we first went to a petrol station and made us ready for what would end up beeing a 6 hour trip to the north
    After we started the motor, I continued with reading a bool called Die Anomalie the book is really interesting and has some very interesting ideas. Therefore I would definitely recommend you the read if you have time for it.
    After I was done with reading I decided to get my mathematics stuff out and started to learn some more number theory in preparation for the next year swiss math Olympiad. In particular I looked at bounding as this is the big topic I havent yet paracticed for the second round next year.
    After I was done studying, I started to listen to some music. The time quickly went by and we soon were at the Fehrmarnsundbridge. From there it was only 10 minutes to the camping place where we resided. On the evening we quickly went to the beach and then soon went sleeping.
